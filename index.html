About the Initiative

The No Block Limit Initiative is an organization dedicated to removing the blocksize limit from Bitcoin Cash.

We believe that the blocksize limit was somthing temporary that satoshi intended to remove, and not to increase it.
We believe that once the blocksize limit is removed, we are guaranteed that bitcoin can and will become cash for the entire world for years to come.



How to Configure your node/client to remove the blocksize limit

Did you know that it is very easy to configure your full node to limit the size of the blocks?
All you have to do is remove the parameter called 'blockmaxsize' in the bitcoin.conf file, which is found in the installation directory of your bitcoin client.
Delete that line, and save the changes to the file, then restart your node.

Cheers! You have successfully removed the blocksize limit from your node.
